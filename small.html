(Toyota Vitz)

Vitz has been the most popular imported car in Pakistan and has dominated the bulk of used car imports ever since. Despite having no warranty and a question market over the availability of spares initially, the Vitz has been the apple-of-eye for Pakistani buyers. Every generation of Vitz have seen some tremendous success in our market. However ironically, Indus Motors the makers of Toyota cars in Pakistan never considered it worthwhile to introduce the popular hatchback in Pakistan.

Toyota (Pixix Epoch)

The Toyota Pixis Epoch is capable of generating a horsepower of 48 HP and a torque of 57 Nm with a top speed of 140 KM/H. Pixis Epoch is available in 1 transmission option(s) i.e Automatic. The Pixis Epoch car has a fuel average of 18 KM/L with a fuel tank capacity of 28L.

(Toyota Passo )

Passo is available in 1 transmission option(s) i.e Automatic. The Passo car has a fuel average of 24 KM/L with a fuel tank capacity of 36L. With the dimensions of 3650 mm in length, 1665 mm in width, and 1525 mm in height ,the Passo has a seating capacity of 5 persons.

Toyota (Aqua)

The Toyota Aqua (Japanese: トヨタ・アクア, Hepburn: Toyota Akua) is a full hybrid electric subcompact/supermini hatchback (B-segment) manufactured and marketed by Toyota since 2011. The nameplate is exclusive to the Japanese domestic market, as the vehicle was exported to overseas markets as the Prius c, leveraging the popularity of the Prius hybrid car nameplate and marketed as the smaller alternative to the Prius liftback.
